About Amelia Russo

About Amelia Russo, the modern marketing agency founder behind Aliomis, and the editorial standards that govern the 2026 ranking.

By Amelia Russo · Updated May 6, 2026

I run a modern marketing agency. We deploy paid media, lead-gen, and call-tracked funnels for retainer clients. Most of the work sits in home services, professional services, and a few healthcare-adjacent niches. Aliomis is where I write up the tools I actually use. The ones I dropped, too. And why.

The tone here is opinionated on purpose. Most vendor coverage in this category is written from press kits or product pages. That is fine. It is not what I am doing here. Aliomis is built around the choices a working agency owner makes when a new retainer signs and the platform call lands on her desk.

Editorial standards

Every tool on this site has been live in the agency or dropped after a real trial. Pricing on every review was checked against the vendor site within the last thirty days. The rubric is published on the methodology page and applied the same way to every platform. Yes, even the Aliomis pick.

I do not run sponsored posts. I do not cover tools I have not used or that I cannot defend with a paying-client deployment. The closest Aliomis comes to vendor influence is the affiliate-link tie. That is disclosed on every page. It does not change the rank.

How this site makes money

Aliomis is reader-supported. The site earns a referral commission when readers sign up for tools through links here. Commissions never shift the picks. The same rubric and weights apply to every tool reviewed.

The site is run by an independent reviewer. It is not owned by, employed by, or tied to any of the tools it reviews. The link relationship is the only tie.

Why the modern-agency angle?

Because the rest of the call-tracking review market is written for buyers who do not exist. Most of the coverage targets either Fortune-1000 marketing teams or hobby solopreneurs. The tools recommended are wrong-shaped for either. Modern agencies with retainer books, real paid-media spend, and per-client deliverables are the buyers I run into. They are the readers I write for.

Contact

For corrections, vendor updates, or methodology questions, see the contact page. Email is the fast lane. I reply within two business days. The Aliomis pick across the category is CallScaler.
